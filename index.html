Mary Dolson, LCSW · Licensed psychotherapist

Trauma-informed counseling, DBT, and EMDR therapy in Portland with telehealth across Oregon for ADHD, trauma, and neurodivergent overwhelm.

Mary Dolson is a Portland-area therapist and Oregon telehealth counselor with a decade of experience supporting adults and families with neurodivergent and trauma-related histories. Her work is rooted in a somatic, trauma-informed approach that helps clients move toward steadiness, self-understanding, and relief.

Outside the therapy room, she loves backyard bird-watching with her trusty bulldog, Beatrice.

Clinical Approaches

Therapeutic approaches

Approaches Mary may draw from depending on your goals, history, and pace.

ACT
Acceptance and Commitment Therapy
Building flexibility, values-based action, and a less reactive relationship to difficult thoughts.
CC
Client-centered Therapy
A collaborative, attuned approach that starts with your pace, your goals, and your lived experience.
CBT
Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
Recognizing thought-behavior patterns and practicing tools that support meaningful change.
DBT
Dialectical Behavioral Therapy
Practical skills for emotion regulation, distress tolerance, mindfulness, and relationship repair.
EMDR
Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing
Helping distressing memories lose intensity so healing feels more possible in the present.
